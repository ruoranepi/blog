Notes from a talk by Kendall Hoyt, ID epi seminar series. During WWII, the world is very productive in developing and improving many vaccines. Many barriers are organizational, not scientific. What are some of the good organizational characteristics that facilitate speedy development? Top down governance Integrated across developmental phases and diciplines Situated in a community that facilitates information transfer CEPI (Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Innovations) - defines the problem as a speed problem.
