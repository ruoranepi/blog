Reading notes: Design Thinking by Nigel Cross

published on August 14, 2018
Nigel Cross: “Design Thinking.” Informative book that helps me to reflect the design (research) process as an independent researcher. Quotes “… designing is not a search for the optimum solution to the given problem, but that it is an exploratory process. The creative designer interprets the design brief not as a specification for a solution, but as a starting point for a journey of exploration; the designer sets off to explore, to discover something new, rather than to reach somewhere already known, or to return with yet another example of the already familiar.
