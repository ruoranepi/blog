The Kirkpatrick Model of learning evaluation: Reaction - what participants thought and felt about the training (satisfaction; “smile sheets”) Learning - the resulting increase in knowledge and/or skills, and change in attitudes. This evaluation occurs during the training in the form of either a knowledge demonstration or test.
